Job Overview:

We are looking for an AI Engineer with a strong background in machine learning, deep learning, and

AI-driven applications. You will be responsible for designing, developing, and deploying AI models,

optimizing algorithms, and integrating LLMs into real-world applications. This role requires practical

experience in AI/ML frameworks and the ability to work in a collaborative, fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:
• Develop and deploy AI/ML models to address business challenges.
• Optimize pre-trained large language models (LLMs) for specific use cases.
• Implement machine learning pipelines, including data preprocessing, model training, and

evaluation.
• Work with structured and unstructured data to derive insights and improve model

performance.
• Collaborate with software engineers to integrate AI models into production systems.
• Keep up to date with advancements in AI, ML, and LLM research.

Required Qualifications:
• Bachelor's or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, or a related field.
• 2+ years of experience working with AI/ML technologies.
• Strong programming skills in Python and experience with ML libraries (TensorFlow, PyTorch,

Scikit-learn, etc.).
• Experience with LLMs (e.g., OpenAI GPT, Gemini, Claude) and NLP techniques.
• Experience with Agentic frameworks like Langgraph, Autogen etc.
• Familiarity with data preprocessing, feature engineering, and model deployment.
• Understanding of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP) and containerization (Docker,

Kubernetes).
• Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work in a collaborative team environment.
